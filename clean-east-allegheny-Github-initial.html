Our Mission

Mind Your Block.
Build your neighborhood.

MindYourBlock is a community-run platform that makes it easy to organize, track, and celebrate neighborhood cleanup efforts in Deutschtown, Pittsburgh.

What We Do

We give Deutschtown residents a simple way to take action. Check into a cleanup zone, log your bags, earn points, and see your impact alongside your neighbors in real time.

Why It Matters

Clean streets build community pride. When neighbors see each other showing up, it creates a culture of care that goes beyond just picking up litter — it makes Deutschtown a better place to live.
